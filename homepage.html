What is this course?

This course extends our understanding of computer systems and computer interfacing with external devices. We will assemble computer systems by installing and configuring appropriate hardware and software, and will learn more about fundamental concepts of electronics, robotics, programming, and networks. We will examine related environmental and societal issues, and will explore postsecondary pathways leading to careers in computer technology.
